How Potassium Nitrate Affects Protein in Mung Beans

This blog explores how different concentrations of potassium nitrate (KNO₃) solution impact the protein concentration in mung bean (Vigna radiata), measured using the Lowry protein assay.

Key focus areas include plant physiology, nutrient absorption, and the importance of nitrogen sources in crop development. The experiment provided insights into how optimal fertilizer concentrations can influence growth and biochemical characteristics of legumes.

By understanding such interactions, we can better optimize agricultural practices for both yield and quality.
